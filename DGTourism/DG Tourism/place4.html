About the area "Dalbeattie"


Facts: Dalbeattie is situated in the Urr valley. Dalbeattie has a meseum devoted to the history of the town and surrounding area. In Dalbeattie you will find 7Stanes Dalbeattie , SandyHills Beach & Dealbeattie woods to explore.



History: The is indicators from court records that lead people to believe that a settlement existed on the site as early as 1658. The beginings of Dalbeattie originate in 1781 when George Maxwell & Alexander Copeland encoureged development of the area by feuing their property, with each feu being a piece of land, which was on a street, and would be large enough to keep a couple farm animals on, as well as build a house on.



Statistics: The population of Dalbeattie is 4'227.
