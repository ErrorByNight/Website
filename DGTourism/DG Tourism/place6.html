Castle Douglas

About the area "Castle Douglas"


Facts: Castle Douglas is built besides Carlingwark Loch, which has traces of prehistoric crannogs, which places evidence of early inhabitation of the area. In Castle Douglas you will find: Galloway Activity Centre , Sulwath Brewers , as well an array of independent shops on the high street.



History: Castle Douglas, was founded in 1792 by William Douglas, He had made his money in an "American Trade", and created a planned town on the shores of Carlingwark Loch. The towns layout is based upon the grid plan of Edinburgh's new town. The town hall was built in 1863 to the designs of Dumfries architecht, James Barbour. The clock tower was buil in 1934-1935, and stands at the corner of King st & St Andrews St.



Statistics: The population of Castle Dougals is 4'174.
