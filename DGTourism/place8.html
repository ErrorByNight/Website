About the area "New Galloway"


Facts: Each August, New Galloway hosts the 'Scottish Alternative Games'. New Galloway is a popular holiday destination, due to the scenic trails on offer. Near New Galloway, you will find The galloway kite trail , A visit to the otters pools at Raiders Road. as well as some nice cafe's and shops in New galloway town.



History: The village was formally founded in the 17th century, by the Viscount of Kenmure, and was granted Royal Burgh status in 1630, this allowed it to serve as a market town. However Kirkcudbright 19 miles to the south was larger and drew more traders. This caused New Galloway to grow very slowly, and become the smallest ROyal Burgh in Scotland.
