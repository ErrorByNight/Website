Welcome to the Walks Page!

We have three main walks, a woodland walk, a field walk and a walk you can take an alpaca on.

The field walk has views over the valley where you can see for miles. You go through fields, make sure to be careful of anything that is growing in them.

The woodland walk has a view of all our trees that drape over the path creating a fantasy feel. We also have an alpaca walk, please email us for more information.
