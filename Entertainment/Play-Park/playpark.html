Welcome to the Play Park Page!

We have a woodland park and a park in a field. These are spread throughout our grounds making it our farm fun for children too.

All playgrounds have padded ground so that there will be less risk of injury. We suggest for children to be 4 and up to go into the parks.

Please do not smoke or bring any dogs into these parks and adult supervision is advised. Thank you.
