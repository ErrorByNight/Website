About the area "Dumfries"


Facts: Dumfries is located 101KM south west of Edinburgh, the capital of scotland. It is the largest town in the county of Dumfries & Galloway. Dumfries has many things to be done such as Wet Jets Jetskiing, Assault Courses , & Murder Mystery trail.



History: In 1186, The scottish king, William the Lion made Dumfries a Royal Burgh. In the late 12th century a castle was built at Dumfries but was demolished in the 14th century. In 1395 Robert III gave Dumfries a charter. By this time, Dumfries was a flourishing market town, as well as an inland port.



Statistics: The population of Dumfries is 31'600.
