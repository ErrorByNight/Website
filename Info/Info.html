Welcome to the Information page!

We are a small farm located between Edinburgh and Carlisle. We have many different things to do on the farm. We have a shop selling local produce and plants, a small petting zoo, a cafe, play parks for the kids and walks.

We open at 9am on a Monday and Tuesday, and 8am on a Wednesday, Thursday, Friday and on the weekends. We are based just outside of Edinburgh. We have beautiful gardens to look around and we have a plant shop to browse, this has some of the plants that we have out in the gardens.
